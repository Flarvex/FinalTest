Depression Support Groups

Depression and Bipolar Support Alliance (DBSA)

Description: DBSA offers peer-led support groups for individuals living with mood disorders, including depression and bipolar disorder.

States: Available in various states nationwide.

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

Description: NAMI provides support groups for individuals and families affected by mental illness, offering a safe space to share experiences.

States: Available in all 50 states.
